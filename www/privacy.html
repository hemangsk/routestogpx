ROUTES TO GPX

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 16, 2026

Summary

We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Routes to GPX runs entirely in your browser. Your routes never leave your device.

How Routes to GPX Works

Routes to GPX uses WebAssembly technology to process your routes directly in your web browser. When you:

  • Paste a Google Maps URL
  • Upload a KML file
  • Preview a route on the map
  • Download a GPX file

All of these operations happen locally on your device. No route data is sent to our servers or any third party.

Data We Don't Collect

  • Route data or GPS coordinates
  • Google Maps URLs you paste
  • KML files you upload
  • GPX files you download
  • Personal information
  • Account information (no accounts required)

Third-Party Services

Map Tiles: When you preview a route, map tiles are loaded from OpenStreetMap tile servers. This is a standard map display and does not include your route data.

Hosting: This website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may collect standard web server logs (IP addresses, browser information) as part of their service. We do not access or use this data.

Cookies

Routes to GPX does not use cookies. We don't track you, and there's nothing to opt out of.

Analytics

We do not use any analytics or tracking tools. We have no insight into how many people use the tool or what routes they convert.

Children's Privacy

Routes to GPX does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
