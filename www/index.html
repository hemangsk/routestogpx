How to Convert Google Maps to GPX

To convert Google Maps to GPX: Paste a Google Maps directions URL or upload a KML file exported from Google My Maps. Click Parse URL or upload your file, preview the route, then Download GPX. Works with Garmin, Wahoo, and all GPS devices. Free, instant, no signup required.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert Google Maps directions to GPX?

Copy your Google Maps directions URL from your browser and paste it into Routes to GPX. Click Parse URL, preview the route on the map, then Download GPX.

How do I convert Google My Maps to GPX?

Open your map at google.com/maps/d, click the three dots menu, select "Export to KML/KMZ", download the KML file, then upload it to Routes to GPX.

Is Routes to GPX free?

Yes, Routes to GPX is completely free with no signup required. All processing happens in your browser using WebAssembly - no data is sent to any server.

What GPS devices work with GPX files?

GPX files work with Garmin Edge, Garmin Forerunner, Wahoo ELEMNT, Wahoo ROAM, Hammerhead Karoo, Komoot, Strava, RideWithGPS, and virtually all GPS cycling computers and watches.

Is my data private?

Yes. All route processing happens directly in your browser using WebAssembly. Your routes are never uploaded to any server. We don't collect, store, or have access to your route data.
